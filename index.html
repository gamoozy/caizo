من قلب القاهرة

From the Heart of Cairo. Shawerma and hawawshi have always been an essential part of Cairo street food culture. They have a lot of similarities with the nature of the city and have a special place in the heart of their lovers as they provoke a lot of memories. They have always been there but never introduced as a brand that can expand and share this experience to new places.
